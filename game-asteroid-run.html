ASTEROID RUN

Dodge the asteroid field and alien fire. Survive as long as you can — your score climbs every second, and things get faster and busier the longer you last.

  • 🖱Mouse / touch: move to steer your ship in any direction.
  • ⌨Keyboard: Arrow keys or WASD to move.
  • ☄Rocks: fall from above — don't let them touch you.
  • 👾Aliens: sweep in from the sides and open fire — dodge the shots, and don't touch the ships either.
  • 🛸Cruiser: after about a minute, a bigger ship shows up and fires a laser — watch for the warning line and move away before it fires.
