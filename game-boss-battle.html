CORE-EATER

A scrap-devouring horror built from the husks of fallen tanks. No fog here — it's always visible, and so are you.

  • ⬛Telegraphed attacks: the boss shows upcoming strike zones as red tiles. Move your tanks off them before its turn.
  • ⬛Easier to dodge = harder it hits. A single cross-shaped strike hurts more than a full-map strike.
  • ⬛3 phases: more simultaneous attack zones appear as its HP drops — plan ahead.
  • ⬛It regenerates if you go 3 turns without landing a hit, and enrages after turn 30.
