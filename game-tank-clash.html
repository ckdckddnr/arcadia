TANK CLASH

Command 4 tanks against an AI squad. Destroy them all, or have the higher combined HP when turns run out.

  • 💎Shared mana: your whole squad shares one pool. It grows +1 each of your turns, up to 10.
  • 🌫️Fog of war: the enemy zone is hidden. Landing a hit reveals that tile only for the rest of the turn.
  • 🎯Move or fire: select a tank, then click an adjacent tile to move, or any enemy-zone tile to fire.
  • 📏Range bonus: the closer your tank is to its target, the more damage it deals — up to +30%.
  • ⚡Skills: Light Tank's Scan reveals a random enemy tank, Medium Tank's Rail Cannon hits hard, Heavy Tank can brace for +50% damage (can't move while braced), and Support Tank can Repair an ally for 300 HP. Each tank can only use its skill once per turn.
