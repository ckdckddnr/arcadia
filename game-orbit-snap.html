ORBIT SNAP

Your ship orbits automatically. Time your launch to slingshot into the next planet.

  • 🖱One button: tap, click, or press space — that's the whole control scheme.
  • 🎯The gate: a glowing arc appears on your orbit. Launch while your ship is inside it to aim true.
  • 🪐Docking: hit the next planet to snap into a new orbit around it and keep going.
  • ⭐Perfect zone: the gold band inside the gate is worth way more — nail it dead-center for a big bonus.
  • 💥Miss the gate and you'll fly off in the wrong direction — one mistake ends the run.
