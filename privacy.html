Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026
This is a placeholder privacy page for a project in development. Before handling real user data at scale, this should be reviewed by a qualified attorney, especially regarding state privacy laws (like CCPA) and payment data handling.

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, a username, and date of birth (to confirm you meet the minimum age requirement). When you play, we store your game scores and the times you played.

2. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show your scores and rank on leaderboards
  • To communicate with you about your account (like password resets)
  • To detect fraud or unfair play

3. Third-party services

We use Supabase for account authentication and data storage. If you sign in with Google or Apple, those providers share basic profile information (like your email) with us to create your account.

4. What we don't do

We don't sell your personal data to advertisers or other third parties.

5. Your choices

You can update your username and password any time from your profile settings. To request account deletion, contact us through the options listed on the site.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
